Privacy Policy

Keep Logged In

Last Updated: November 2025

Keep Logged In does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All functionality occurs locally on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No browsing history
  • No personal information
  • No passwords or credentials
  • No analytics
  • No tracking of any kind

How the Extension Works

The extension only performs local actions such as:

  • Keeping browser tabs active
  • Sending local activity signals to prevent site timeouts
  • Optionally refreshing a tab if enabled by the user
  • Saving simple settings locally, such as on or off state and timer interval

Permissions

The permissions requested are used only to keep sessions active and do not access or transmit any data.

Third Party Sharing

None. No data is collected, so none can be shared.
